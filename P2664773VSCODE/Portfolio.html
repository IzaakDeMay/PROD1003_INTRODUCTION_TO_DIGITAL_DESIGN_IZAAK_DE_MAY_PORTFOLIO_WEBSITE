Photography and Film Work

Wooden Plank

The above image was taken whilst on a walk in the east of Suffolk here in the UK. East Suffolk is known for its wildlife areas and This was taken on a nature walk in the area.

Evening Sky

This particular image was taken whilst on an evening walk by the seaside. When looking around the beautiful scenery I discovered the full beauty of the sky.

Lone Pinecone

This particular image was taken whilst on another nature walk in the east of Suffolk. Whilst in the woodlands, I noticed this lone pinecone which had perfectly fallen into that position which gave me the opportunity to take that photograph.

Scar in the Wall

This particular image was taken whilst out in a small town in Suffolk called Woodbridge where whilst exploring the area I discovered this abandoned house which has this peculiar mark in the wall. Fascinated by what I was seeing I thought why not capture the moment and see what I could do it creatively in Photoshop.

The Forgotten Glove

This particular image was taken exploring the woodlands of East Suffolk where whilst walking through the nature walks, this gate had an old forgotten glove which someone had dropped. Seeing this glove gave I was inspired to photograph it to tell the story of the abandoned glove.

The Trees Protection

TThis particular image was taken around the old village/estate I used live in and in the time leading up to this image I was experimenting with different angles on tree barks and that's when this image was photographed.

Sugar Pot

When exploring the streets of Woodbridge, there was a particular cafe which had some outdoor seating. After passing one of the tables I had noticed these fancy looking sugar pots that they had on offer for the customers. After playing around with the settings and angles, This image then became the result.

Aging Nail

Whilst exploring a historic windmill in the East of Suffol I was observing the walls and structure of the windmill. This is when I had discovered this rusty old nail which looked like the perfect thing to photograph. With an black and white effect placed on the image this is what had resulted.

Time

The above image was taken whilst during lockdown where I was experimenting with the spring sky view in my own back garden. Whilst experimenting with different angles this is one of the results of that experimentation.
